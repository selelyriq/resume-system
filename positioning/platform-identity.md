# Platform Engineering Identity

## Focus Areas

- System design and architecture
- Terraform architecture and modularity
- Blast radius isolation
- Reusable, composable infrastructure
- Multi-account strategy
- Observability standards and implementation
- Infrastructure as a product mindset

## Core Narrative

**I design scalable infrastructure systems that reduce operational and organizational risk.**

The goal is not just building infrastructure—it's building it in ways that scale safely. That means thinking about blast radius, modularity, team ownership, and observability from the first line of Terraform.

## Key Stories

- **Terraform State Isolation**: Designed logical state segmentation across app/account/env to enable parallel team ownership while minimizing blast radius
- **Github Enterprise Migration**: Led safe migration of 400+ repos with minimal disruption through coordinated, planned transitions
- **Internal Developer Platform**: Productized standardized environment using Dev Containers, reducing onboarding friction from 1-2 days to 30 minutes
- **$80M/day Cutover**: Designed and executed high-risk cloud migration with zero downtime for mission-critical system

## Long-Term Direction

Platform thinking (systems design, reusability, operational safety) is the core identity. This is the primary positioning.
